Frog LifeTM

How to Play:

  • Turn your mobile device sideways and zoom out.
  • Use WASD to move.
  • Click blocks to pick them up with your tongue!
  • Select items in inventory and click to place!
    (Click the item again to deselect)
  • Find the treasure!
